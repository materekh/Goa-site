About us

    Our academy is friendly and trustworthy. we teach you in a way that's best for you and help you develop mentally and physically. Our mission is to help people from every age group learn programming. Our lessons are held on a discord server. Every week we have lessons. We learn in an interactive way. When you join you have to choose a clan. If you have questions you can contact your clan leader.


Classifications

1) CEO. CEO of the academy is the highest priority. CEO runs the academy.

2) Mentor. Mentor teaches students.

3) Moderator. Moderstors do what CEO and teachers tell them. Moderator manages the academy.

4) Leader. Leaders do what moderators, CEO and mentors tell them. Leaders have a clan and their job is to help their clan.

5) Mini leader. Mini leaders help leaders do their job.


Other classificstions

• Mini mentor. Mini mentor teaches people individually.

• Github checker. Github checker checks people's githubs for homework.
